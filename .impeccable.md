## Design Context

### Design Vision: "The Ethereal Navigator"

#### Creative North Star
To transform the chaotic process of job hunting into a serene, guided experience. The AI feels like a sophisticated assistant whispering insights through frosted glass, rather than a robotic tool spitting out data. 

*   **Spatial UI:** We prioritize breathing room, intentional asymmetry, and depth over dense grids and heavy borders.
*   **Intelligence through Vibrancy:** Vibrant accents are used sparingly to communicate AI-generated insights and high-match scores.

#### Tonal Architecture (Colors)
*   **Background (`#f6fafe`):** The base canvas.
*   **Surface Layering (The Depth Engine):** Instead of box-shadows, depth is conveyed by layering surface tiers. 
    *   `surface_container_lowest` (`#ffffff`): High-priority floating cards.
    *   `surface_container_low` (`#f0f4f8`): Secondary content sections.
    *   `surface_container_highest` (`#dfe3e7`): Recessed elements (search bars, inactive tabs).
*   **Signature AI Gradient:** A 45-degree linear gradient from `primary` (#630ed4) to `secondary` (#0058be). Reserved for AI highlights and primary actions.
*   **No-Line Rule:** Strictly avoid 1px solid borders for sectioning. Use surface-tonal shifts and spacing (`spacing-8`) to create organic boundaries.

#### Visual Polish & Elevation
*   **Glass & Gradient Rule:** Modals and nav elements use `surface_container_lowest` at 70% opacity with `20px` backdrop-blur.
*   **Ambient Shadows:** Floating elements (like match score popovers) use soft, barely-there shadows: `0 8px 32px rgba(23, 28, 31, 0.04)`.
*   **Ghost Borders:** When a container sits on a background of the same color, use `outline_variant` at 20% opacity to suggest an edge without defining it.

#### Fluid Primitives
*   **Buttons:** 
    *   *Primary*: Gradient (primary → primary_container), 12px radius, no border.
    *   *Secondary*: `surface_container_highest` background, resembling a soft pebble.
    *   *State*: 10% white overlay on hover to simulate an inner glow.
*   **Inputs:** Background `surface_container_low` shifting to `surface_container_lowest` on focus. Subtle 20% opacity outline.
*   **Match Cards:** Strictly no internal dividers. Use `1.5rem` spacing between title and metadata. AI Match Scores use a circular glass chip with an inner shadow glow.

### Users
High-achieving tech professionals (ML/AI/DS) navigating a complex and often stressful job search. They are data-driven, value efficiency, and need a disciplined system to turn scattered listings into a structured application pipeline.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice/Tone**: Authoritative yet calm, efficient, and intentional.
- **Personality**: The Ethereal Navigator — a reliable copilot that removes noise and focuses on action.
- **Emotional Goals**: Calm/Reliable, Productive/Disciplined.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Spatial UI with Refined Minimalism.
- **References**: High-performance utilities like Linear, Raycast, and Cron, fused with editorial layouts and high-end glass interfaces.
- **Theme**: Both Light and Dark modes supported with tonal layering.
- **Key Elements**: Tonal depth (instead of drop-shadows), asymmetrical layouts, and "Ethereal" glass effects.

### Design Principles
1. **Systems over Surfaces**: Design for workflows and state transitions, not just static screens.
2. **Information Density with Clarity**: Maintain high signal-to-noise ratio. Use hierarchy to guide attention to the next best action.
3. **Calm Authority**: Reduce job-search anxiety through structured data, reliable processing feedback, and a clean, spacious aesthetic.
4. **Performance as a Feature**: The interface should feel as fast and responsive as the utilities tech professionals use daily.

#### Do's and Don'ts
*   **Do** use asymmetrical layouts. A job description can be 60% width while the "AI Copilot Sidebar" is 35%, leaving a 5% "negative space" gap.
*   **Do** use `9999px` (Full) roundness for status chips and match scores, but stick to `12px` (md) for containers.
*   **Do** use letter-spacing of `-0.01em` on all text above 16px to give it a tighter, more "Apple-esque" finish.
*   **Don't** use pure black (#000000). Always use `on_surface` (#171c1f) for text to maintain the soft-light aesthetic.
*   **Don't** use a divider line to separate a header from a body. Use a `spacing-8` gap or a subtle background tonal shift.
*   **Don't** clutter the screen. If an element isn't vital to the "Current Task," it should have a reduced opacity (e.g., 60%) until hovered.
